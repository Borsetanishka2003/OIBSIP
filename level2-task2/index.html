A few things about K.K.

His real name is Totakeke, but he uses the name K.K. Slider when he plays on stage. His music is always chill and so is his personality. Although he carries an acoustic guitar, his instrumentation ranges far beyond a single instrument. He is a creative musician who finds inspiration from numerous sources.
